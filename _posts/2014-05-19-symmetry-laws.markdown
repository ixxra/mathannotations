Let $O$ be an observable in $L_2(X)$. If $O$ is independent from time, then 

$$\frac{dO}{dt} = [O, H].$$

In particular, if $O$ commutes with the system hamiltonian, we get the celebrated *Noether's Theorem*, in his symplectic version.
